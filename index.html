About ICNC 2026

The IEEE International Conference on Intelligent Computing, Networking, and Communications (ICNC 2026) aims to bring together leading academic scientists, researchers, and research scholars to exchange and share their experiences and research results on all aspects of Intelligent Computing, Networking, and Communications. It also provides a premier interdisciplinary platform for researchers, practitioners, and educators to present and discuss the most recent innovations, trends, and concerns as well as practical challenges encountered and solutions adopted in the fields.

Call for Papers

We invite submissions of original, unpublished research work. Topics of interest include, but are not limited to:

Area 1: Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
  • Deep Learning and Neural Networks
  • Reinforcement Learning
  • Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition
  • Natural Language Processing
  • AI for Networking and Communications
Area 2: Next-Generation Networking and IoT Communication Systems
  • 6G and Future Networks
  • Software-Defined Networking (SDN) and Network Function Virtualization (NFV)
  • Internet of Things (IoT) Architectures and Protocols
  • Wireless Communication Systems
  • Network Slicing and Resource Management
Area 3: Cybersecurity and Privacy
  • Blockchain and Distributed Ledger Technology
  • Intrusion Detection and Prevention
  • AI-driven Security
  • Privacy-Enhancing Technologies
  • Network and Cloud Security
Area 4: Smart Systems and Edge Computing
  • Edge/Fog Computing Architectures
  • Mobile Edge Intelligence
  • Smart City Systems and Applications
  • Cloud-Edge Collaboration
  • Distributed and Federated Learning at the Edge
